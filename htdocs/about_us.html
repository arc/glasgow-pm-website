About Glasgow Perl Mongers

What is Glasgow.pm?

Glasgow Perl Mongers is a group of people who use Perl in their work or personal programming projects, and like to meet and talk about it.
 

How do I get in touch?

Contact us via the mailing list.
If you want to get in touch with the leader, e-mail mfontani at cpan dot org.
