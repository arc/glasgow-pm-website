Glasgow Perl Mongers Meetings

When do meetings take place?

The group has not yet decided on a specific meeting date, but in order to not co-incide with the timing of the Edinburgh.pm's meetings (fourth Thursday of the month) it is likely meetings will be held on the second week of the month, possibly on Thursdays.
As soon as a decision is made, this section will contain the next three meetings' dates.
 

Next meeting

As discussed on the mailing list, our next meeting (our first technical meeting) will be on Thursday 2010-11-11 at 18:15.
 

Where do technical meetings take place?

The group has been given access to a room/projector at S1's offices in Glasgow, located at 200 Renfield street (Google maps link).
 

What kind of meetings should I expect?

The aim is to have predominantly technical meetings, with the odd social meeting once in a while. The timing of the meetings does also allow members from nearby Perl Mongers group to participate.
If you're interested, please join the discussion on the mailing list to voice your opinion.
 

Where are the meetings slides?

Heh, we'd have to have a meeting first! The aim is to either host the slides on this server, or to have specific pages for each of our technical meetings and -- providing the author doesn't mind -- the slides will be available shortly after the meeting.
